Commonly Asked Questions

Does it cost to join?

NOPE! Everything is totally free!

How do I join?

You can join the discussions on either Discord, or Instagram! Our discord server is where most of the action happens!

How do I participate?

How you participate is totally up to you. Every month we will pick a new book and topic. If that book interests you, then give it a read! If the book isn't something you're interested in, then skip this month. Once you finish a book, tell us what you thought and learned and join the discussion.
