Commonly Asked Questions

Does it cost to join?

NOPE! Everything is totally free!

How do I join?

You can join the discussions, quizzes, giveaways and more on either Discord, or Instagram, or both!

How do I participate?

How you participate is totally up to you. Every month we will pick a new book and topic. If that book interests you, then give it a read! If the book isn't something you're interested in, then skip that month.

Once you finish the month's book, tell us what you thought and learned and join in on the discussion, take the quizzes, enter the giveaways and have fun!
